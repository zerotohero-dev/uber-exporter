# Ideas

Early-stage thoughts, explorations, and proposals.

Ideas here may or may not be ready for public discussion. This is a
low-pressure space for capturing possibilities before they're refined.

## When to .gitignore this folder

Consider adding `ideas/` to `.gitignore` if:

- You have many ideas and don't want to create premature discussions
- You prefer to keep your thinking private until it's ready for review
- You're working in a public repo and ideas might be misread as commitments

## Graduating an idea

When an idea reaches a reviewable state, promote it to `specs/`:

1. Formalize the problem, solution, and non-goals
2. Move to `specs/` (or write a fresh spec inspired by the idea)
3. Add a Phase to TASKS.md referencing the spec

Ideas that stay here indefinitely are fine: not everything needs to ship.
